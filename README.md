# Hotel-Bookings-Performance-Analysis
Power BI dashboard analyzing hotel booking and revenue performance, cancellations, and guest behavior. The project provides insights into occupancy rates, booking trends, and customer patterns to support data-driven decision-making in hospitality management.

# Technical Steps 
1. **connect to power bi**
- Open Power BI Desktop.
- Connect to the hotel_bookings.csv file.
- Perform data cleaning and transformation if necessary (e.g., handling missing values, renaming columns).

2.  **Make Two Dashboards**
**Booking & Revenue Performance**
**Total Booking** :  Use a card visual to display total booking.
- **Total Revenue**:  Use a card visual to display total revenue.
- **ADR**:  Use a card visual to display ADR.
- **AVG length od stay**:  Use a card visual to display AVG length od stay.
- **AVG lead time**: Use a card visual to display AVG lead time
- **Monthly trend** : Use line and clustred column chart to show Booking, Cancellations Performance across months
- **Revenue & ADR by year and month**: Use line and clustred column chart to show Distribution of Revenue & ADR across years and months
- **Top 10 Countries by booking** : Use stacked bar chart to show Analysis of booking for top countries

**Cancellations & Guest Behavior**
- **canceled bookings**: Use a card visual to display canceled bookings
- **cancellation ratio** :  Use a card visual to display cancellation ratio
- **Repeat guest**:  Use a card visual to display Repeat guest
- **Relized nights**  Use a card visual to display Relized nights
- **No show** :  Use a card visual to display No show%
- **Top 10 Countries by cancellation** :  Use stacked bar chart to display Top 10 Countries by cancellation
- **Canceled Bookings by deposit type**: Use pie chart to diplay Canceled Bookings by deposit type
- **Canceled Bookings by Market Segment**:Use stacked bar chart to display Canceled Bookings by Market Segment
- **Canceled Bookings by Distribution Channel** : Use stacked bar chart to display Canceled Bookings by Distribution Channel
- **Length of stay by lead time**: Use line chart to display Length of stay by lead time
